  • \(A\left(1\,;\,\,-\dfrac{1}{5}\right)\)

  • \(B\left(0\,;\,\,1\right)\)

  • \(C\left(\dfrac{1}{2}\,;\,\,0\right)\)

  • \(D\left(-\dfrac{7}{6}\,;\,\,\dfrac{2}{3}\right)\)

  • \(E\left(0.3\,;\,\,-3\right)\)


Soluzione: I punti \(A\), \(C\), \(D\) apparengono alla retta \(r\), gli altri no.
